Realistic Military Vehicles Pack v1.0

This pack contains 5 AAA quality  assets perfect for realistic war games. Each vehicle includes a diffuse, metallic and normal map, separated components such as turrets for full control over animations and are prefabed for ease of use.

Each asset also comes with two different textures, a clean one and a battle weathered one which can be switched around.
